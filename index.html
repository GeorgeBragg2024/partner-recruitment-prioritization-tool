Potential levels of engagement
Typically the more mature the partner, the less they want or need deeper support
Level 1
  • Share existing candidates + make intros
Level 2
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source (networking & online)
Level 3
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source + initial screen
Level 4
  • Share existing candidates
  • Source, screen, full interviews
  • Process support
Level 5
  • Full end-to-end responsibility
Note: Light process support (e.g. interview questions, scorecard design) can be offered as a standalone at any level — it is not tied to a sourcing engagement.
